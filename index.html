MemoryIO creates a log of your life by taking a photo every time you open your Mac.

View details »

When memoryIO is in your tray, you can force a photo or get one when you wake up from sleep.

Click the notification to see the photo and, if you like, tweet it.

All photos are automatically saved to your My Pictures directory

Open Source

The source code to MemoryIO is distributed under the GNU GPLv3 license and is currently hosted on github.

To start working with the source, first install git. Then run:

git clone git://github.com/augmentous/memoryio-mac.git
